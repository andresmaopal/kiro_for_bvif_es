# Metrics Identification

| # | Business Value Category | Metric Name | Arithmetic Definition | Explanation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Grow Revenue | Campaign Conversion Rate* | (Number of corporate prospects that became new loan clients ÷ Total corporate prospects contacted in campaign) × 100 | Measures how effectively AI-improved targeting identifies the right corporate prospects. Higher conversion means outreach effort translates into actual lending relationships. |
| 2 | Grow Revenue | New Corporate Loans Originated* | Count of new corporate loan contracts signed per quarter | Directly tracks topline growth in the corporate lending book. More loans originated means more interest income generated. |
| 3 | Grow Revenue | Average Time-to-Decision | Average number of business days from complete corporate credit application submission to final credit decision | Faster decisions reduce client attrition to competitors. A shorter cycle means BGR wins more deals in competitive situations. |
| 4 | Grow Revenue | Corporate Lending Revenue (Interest Income) | Total interest income from corporate loan portfolio per quarter (USD) | The ultimate revenue metric — captures the financial outcome of originating more corporate loans through better targeting and faster decisions. |
| 5 | Increase Operational Efficiency | Cost per Credit Decision* | Total credit origination operational cost (analyst salaries + overhead allocated to origination) ÷ Number of corporate credit decisions issued per quarter | Measures how efficiently the bank produces each credit decision. AI automation should reduce the labor hours per decision, lowering this cost. |
| 6 | Increase Operational Efficiency | Analyst Productivity (Decisions per Analyst) | Number of corporate credit decisions issued per quarter ÷ Number of FTE credit analysts | Captures whether the same team can handle more volume. Higher productivity means scalable capacity without proportional headcount growth. |
| 7 | Increase Operational Efficiency | Manual Processing Hours per Application | Total analyst hours spent on document gathering, financial analysis, and credit memo preparation ÷ Number of corporate applications processed | Tracks the direct labor reduction from AI automating structured, repetitive origination tasks. |

---

**Notes:**
- Metrics marked with `*` are considered most representative for executive follow-up.
- All metrics are expressed as absolute numbers, percentages, or currency amounts.
- Feasibility assessment (whether data exists to track these today) will be addressed in Task 2.
