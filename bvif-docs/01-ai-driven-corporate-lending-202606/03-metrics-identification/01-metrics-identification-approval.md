# Stage 3 / Task 1 — Metrics Identification Approval

## What you're approving
The list of metrics we propose using to prove the initiative delivered value. For each business value category from Stage 2, we've identified metrics that are directly influenced by the initiative, meaningful to the budget owner, and expressible as an absolute number, a percentage, or a currency amount. Metrics marked with an asterisk (*) are the ones we consider most representative.

At this point we are only deciding **what to measure**, not whether each metric is easy to measure today — feasibility comes next in Task 2, and adjustments (proxies, activation, deferral) come in Stage 4. Approving means you agree these metrics are the right ones to tell the story of the initiative's value.
