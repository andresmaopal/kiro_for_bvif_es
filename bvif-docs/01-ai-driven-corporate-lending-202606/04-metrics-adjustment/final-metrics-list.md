# Final Metrics List

All 7 metrics proceed to Stage 5 (Data Collection). No metrics were eliminated, deferred, or replaced.

| # | Business Value Category | Metric Name | Arithmetic Definition | Explanation | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Grow Revenue | Campaign Conversion Rate* | (Corporate prospects that became loan clients ÷ Total prospects contacted) × 100 | Measures AI-improved targeting effectiveness — higher conversion means outreach reaches the right corporate prospects. | Ready (F1) |
| 2 | Grow Revenue | New Corporate Loans Originated* | Count of new corporate loan contracts per quarter | Directly tracks topline growth in the corporate lending book — more loans = more interest income. | Ready (F1) |
| 3 | Grow Revenue | Average Time-to-Decision | Average business days from complete application to final credit decision | Faster decisions reduce client loss to competitors — the bank that says "yes" first wins the relationship. | Ready (F1) |
| 4 | Grow Revenue | Corporate Lending Revenue (Interest Income) | Total interest income from corporate loan portfolio per quarter (USD) | The ultimate revenue outcome — captures financial impact of originating more corporate loans. | Ready (F1) |
| 5 | Increase Operational Efficiency | Cost per Credit Decision* | Total origination cost (analyst salaries + overhead) ÷ Number of corporate credit decisions per quarter | Measures cost efficiency — AI automation should lower labor hours per decision, reducing this cost. | Activate (F2) |
| 6 | Increase Operational Efficiency | Analyst Productivity (Decisions per Analyst) | Corporate credit decisions per quarter ÷ FTE credit analysts | Captures scalable capacity — same team handles more volume without proportional headcount growth. | Ready (F1) |
| 7 | Increase Operational Efficiency | Manual Processing Hours per Application | Total analyst hours on document gathering + financial analysis + credit memo ÷ Applications processed | Direct measure of automation impact — tracks labor reduction from AI handling structured, repetitive work. | Activate (F2) |
