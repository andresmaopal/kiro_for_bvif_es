# Stage 2 — Business Value Mapping Approval

## What you're approving
Which of the four AWS Cloud Adoption Framework (CAF v3) business value categories best describe how this initiative delivers value. Picking the right category (or two) shapes the metrics we'll choose in Stage 3 and how we'll express the financial impact in Stage 6. Different categories get compared differently — for example, revenue growth needs a margin adjustment before it can be compared with operational savings.

### The four CAF v3 business value categories
- **Grow Revenue** — topline growth. Increases in customer consumption, new customers, or expansion into new addressable markets. Revenue impact requires a contribution-margin adjustment before it's comparable to cost savings.
- **Increase Operational Efficiency** — bottom-line savings. Either cost reduction (lowering existing expenses) or cost avoidance (preventing future costs). Directly comparable to other cost-savings figures.
- **Reduce Business Risk** — investments in regulatory compliance, security, resilience, and similar areas. Usually no direct financial benefit, but prevents material downside (fines, outages, data loss).
- **Improve ESG** — Environmental, Social, or Governance objectives. No direct financial benefit, but delivers strategic and reputational value aligned with sustainability and responsibility commitments.

Want the full definitions and examples? See the AWS Cloud Adoption Framework landing page: https://aws.amazon.com/cloud-adoption-framework/
