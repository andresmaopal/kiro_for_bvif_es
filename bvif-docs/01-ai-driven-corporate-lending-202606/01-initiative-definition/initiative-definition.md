# Initiative Definition — AI-Driven Corporate Lending

## Who We Are

Banco General Rumiñahui (BGR) is a mid-size retail bank with approximately USD 3,000 million in assets. The bank serves both retail and corporate customers, with a corporate lending portfolio that is central to its growth strategy.

## What We Propose

We propose implementing an AI-driven solution to transform our corporate credit origination process — from prospect identification through credit decision. The initiative will use artificial intelligence to improve how we identify the right corporate prospects for lending campaigns, assess their creditworthiness, and deliver faster, more consistent credit decisions.

## Current State

Today, our corporate credit origination is largely manual. Analysts gather documentation from corporate applicants, manually review financial statements, and produce credit memos with limited automation support. This process has three critical challenges:

- **It's too slow.** Corporate clients wait too long for credit decisions. In a competitive banking market, this delay risks losing valuable clients to faster-moving competitors.
- **Risk assessment is inconsistent.** Different analysts reviewing similar corporate profiles can reach different conclusions, leading to uneven risk exposure and missed opportunities.
- **Operational cost is high.** Each corporate credit decision requires significant analyst hours, limiting how many applications we can process with our current team.

Additionally, we are not effectively identifying the right corporate prospects for our lending campaigns. Our current targeting approach results in outreach to companies that don't match our ideal credit profile, wasting commercial effort and missing better-fit opportunities.

## Future State

With AI in place, our corporate lending process will be fundamentally different:

- **Faster decisions**: Corporate clients will receive credit decisions in significantly less time, improving client satisfaction and our competitive position.
- **Consistent, data-driven assessment**: AI will provide standardized risk evaluation across all corporate applications, reducing analyst-to-analyst variability.
- **Lower cost per decision**: Automation of document analysis and preliminary scoring will reduce the manual hours required per application, allowing our team to handle more volume.
- **Smarter prospecting**: AI will identify the right corporate targets for lending campaigns, improving conversion rates and ensuring our commercial effort reaches companies that match our risk appetite and growth objectives.
- **Scalable capacity**: The same team will be able to serve more corporate clients without proportional headcount growth.

## Business Impact Areas

1. **Revenue growth** — Better prospect targeting and faster decisions mean more corporate clients close, directly contributing to the bank's growth mandate.
2. **Operational efficiency** — Reduced cost per credit decision and higher analyst productivity free resources for complex, high-value cases.
3. **Risk quality** — Consistent, data-driven assessments reduce the variability in credit decisions and improve portfolio health.

## Budget Ownership

The **Gerente de Crédito (Credit VP)** is the initiative sponsor and primary budget owner. This role is directly responsible for the performance of the credit origination function — speed, volume, quality, and cost. The initiative's benefits directly improve the outcomes this role is measured on.

Additional stakeholders with strong alignment:
- **CEO / President**: Measured on overall bank growth — faster, smarter corporate lending directly contributes to topline expansion.
- **CIO**: Measured on technology's business impact — this initiative is a clear demonstration of technology driving measurable business outcomes.
