# Metrics Identification

| # | Business Value Category | Metric Name | Arithmetic Definition | Explanation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Grow Revenue | Sócio Annual Renewal Rate %* | (Members active on Jan 1 still active on Dec 31 / Members active on Jan 1) × 100 | Headline indicator of membership stickiness. The model is ultimately optimizing this number. |
| 2 | Grow Revenue | Members Exited per Year (Voluntary + Lapsed)* | Count of exits in a calendar year, split into voluntary cancellations and payment-failure lapses | Operational counterpart to renewal rate; expressed in absolute numbers it makes the churn problem tangible (36,000 total exits in 2025: 19,800 voluntary + 16,200 lapsed). |
| 3 | Grow Revenue | Sócio Membership Revenue $ | Sum of monthly fees collected from active members during the period | Direct topline revenue impact of retention performance. |
| 4 | Grow Revenue | Average Match-Day Spend per Sócio Fan $ | Total match-day F&B + merchandise spend by sócio fans / Sócio fan attendance | Captures the compounding effect: long-tenured high-attendance sócios spend more on match day than non-members. |
| 5 | Increase Operational Efficiency | Avoided Acquisition Cost $ | (Members retained vs. baseline scenario) × Blended CAC ($47) | Quantifies the operational efficiency benefit: retained members do not need to be re-acquired. |
| 6 | Grow Revenue | Net Promoter Score (NPS) | NPS standard formula | Member satisfaction proxy; commonly tracked but not a direct BVIF lever for this initiative. Listed for completeness. |

**Highlighted (most relevant for executive follow-up):** Metrics 1 and 2.

**Notes:**
- All metrics are absolute number, percentage, or currency, per BVIF rules.
- Metric 6 (NPS) is a candidate for elimination at Stage 4 — it does not change the financial calculation.
