# Metrics Feasibility Assessment

Feasibility levels:
- **F1** — Metric already tracked and reported regularly.
- **F2** — Underlying data exists but the metric is not currently calculated/reported.
- **F3** — Data is missing, partial, or unreliable.

| # | Metric | Feasibility | Comment |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Sócio Annual Renewal Rate % | **F1** | Already calculated annually and reported in board materials. Quarterly reporting introduced in 2024 with BOLALAKE. |
| 2 | Members Exited per Year (Voluntary + Lapsed) | **F1** | Tracked daily in the membership system; the voluntary/lapsed split (now visible since the 2024 BOLALAKE retrospective) is aggregated to monthly and annual reports. |
| 3 | Sócio Membership Revenue $ | **F1** | Reported monthly via finance close. |
| 4 | Average Match-Day Spend per Sócio Fan $ | **F2** | Required POS-to-fan-ID linkage, which is now available via BOLALAKE. Metric not yet operationalized as a recurring report. |
| 5 | Avoided Acquisition Cost $ | **F2** | Acquisition channel mix and per-channel CAC are available in BOLALAKE; the avoided-cost calculation is not currently produced. |
| 6 | Net Promoter Score (NPS) | **F3** | Surveyed annually but response rates are low (~7%) and the methodology has changed twice in three years. Not reliable as a comparison metric. |

## Summary
- F1: 3 metrics (Renewal Rate, Cancellations, Revenue)
- F2: 2 metrics (Match-Day Spend per Sócio, Avoided CAC) — both have data; need calculation cadence.
- F3: 1 metric (NPS) — recommend elimination at Stage 4.
