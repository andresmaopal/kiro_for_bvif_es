# Session Context

## Customer
- **Name**: Clube Esportivo Bandeirantes ("Os Bandeirantes")
- **Industry**: Professional sports / Brazilian football (Série A)
- **Location**: São Bernardo das Minas, Minas Gerais, Brazil
- **Scale**: 6.2M registered supporters; 75,000 active sócio members; $206.8M operating revenue (2025, excl. transfers)
- **Note**: Fictional, used for BVIF framework testing.

## AI Initiative Under Evaluation
**Membership Churn Prediction** — an AI model that monitors sócio member behavior continuously (attendance, app engagement, payment history, tier changes) and identifies members at risk of cancelling before they do, enabling targeted retention outreach.

## Sponsor / Budget Owner
**Chief Digital Officer (CDO) — Marcos Alves**, with the **Chief Commercial Officer** sharing accountability for membership retention outcomes.

## Selection Rationale (for the proof run)
This initiative was selected because its primary value lever — sócio renewal rate — maps cleanly to the Grow Revenue category and exercises the full BVIF process: numeric metrics, 8-quarter historical data, clear financial impact per unit ($541 per retained member), industry benchmarks, and a defensible improvement target range.
