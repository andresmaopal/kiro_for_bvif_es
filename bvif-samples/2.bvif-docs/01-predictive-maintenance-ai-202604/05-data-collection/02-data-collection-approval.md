# Stage 5 — Data Collection Approval

## Data Collection Summary

Data has been collected for all 7 finalized metrics, including current baselines, historical trends (8 quarters), financial impact analysis, industry benchmarks, and realistic improvement targets.

### Key Findings:

**Operational Efficiency Metrics:**
1. **Unplanned Downtime Rate**: 2.8% current → 1.0-1.4% target (vs. 1.5% industry benchmark)
2. **MTBF**: 4,114 hours current → 5,554-5,965 hours target (vs. 6,000+ hours industry benchmark)
3. **Maintenance Cost per Unit**: $12.00 current → $8.40-$9.60 target (vs. $9.50-$10.50 industry benchmark)
4. **Unplanned Work Order Rate**: 15.0% current → 8.0% target (vs. <8% industry benchmark)
5. **Planned/Unplanned Ratio**: 5.67:1 current → 11.5:1 target (vs. 11.5:1 industry benchmark)

**Revenue Growth Metrics:**
6. **On-Time Delivery Rate**: 90.0% current → 95.0% target (vs. 95%+ industry benchmark)
7. **Production Capacity Utilization**: 84.4% current → 92.2-94.9% target (vs. 92-95% industry benchmark)

### Financial Context:
- Annual production: 720,000 units
- Average revenue per unit: $250
- Contribution margin: 46%
- Lost contribution margin per downtime hour: $9,971
- Current annual maintenance costs: $8,640,000
- Emergency reactive repair costs: $2,880,000 (33.3% of total maintenance)

### Historical Trends:
All metrics show deterioration over the past 2 years:
- Unplanned downtime increased from 2.5-2.7% to 2.8-3.0%
- MTBF declined 12% from 4,680 hours to 4,114 hours
- Maintenance cost per unit increased 10.6% from $10.85 to $12.00
- Reactive maintenance rate increased 8.7% from 13.8% to 15.0%
- OTD rate declined 2.4 percentage points from 92.4% to 90.0%
- Capacity utilization declined from 85.6% to 84.4%

### Improvement Targets Rationale:
All targets are based on:
- Industry benchmarks from Woodworking Machinery Industry Association
- Peer case studies showing 20-30% maintenance cost reductions
- Pilot data from similar manufacturers achieving 50-65% downtime reduction
- Best-in-class performance levels for mature predictive maintenance programs
