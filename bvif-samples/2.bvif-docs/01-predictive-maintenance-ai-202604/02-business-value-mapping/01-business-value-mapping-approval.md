# Stage 2 — Business Value Mapping Approval

## Business Value Categories Selected

### Primary Category: Increase Operational Efficiency

**Reasoning**: This predictive maintenance initiative directly reduces operational costs and prevents future expenses by:
- Reducing unplanned downtime to increase manufacturing throughput and capacity utilization
- Optimizing maintenance scheduling to reduce emergency repair costs and extend equipment lifespan
- Preventing catastrophic failures to avoid expensive emergency repairs and replacement costs

The core value is cost avoidance (preventing downtime costs) and cost savings (optimized maintenance), which directly align with operational efficiency improvements.

### Secondary Category: Grow Revenue

**Reasoning**: There is a secondary revenue growth component through improved delivery reliability and on-time fulfillment:
- Strengthening customer retention through consistent delivery performance
- Enabling capacity for growth by creating headroom to take on additional orders without new equipment investment
- Improving competitive positioning through reliable delivery commitments

This revenue impact is real but secondary to the direct operational efficiency gains, as it depends on converting improved reliability into customer acquisition and retention outcomes.
