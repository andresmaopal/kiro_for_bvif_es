# Business Value Mapping

## Primary Category: Increase Operational Efficiency

This predictive maintenance initiative directly reduces operational costs and prevents future expenses. By shifting from reactive to proactive maintenance, we will:
- **Reduce unplanned downtime**: Minimizing production line stoppages increases manufacturing throughput and capacity utilization
- **Optimize maintenance scheduling**: Performing maintenance at optimal times reduces emergency repair costs and extends equipment lifespan
- **Prevent catastrophic failures**: Early detection of potential issues avoids expensive emergency repairs and replacement costs

The core value proposition is cost avoidance (preventing unplanned downtime costs) and cost savings (optimized maintenance activities), which directly align with operational efficiency improvements.

## Secondary Category: Grow Revenue

While the primary impact is operational efficiency, there is a secondary revenue growth component. By improving delivery reliability and on-time fulfillment through reduced downtime, we:
- **Strengthen customer retention**: Consistent delivery performance builds trust and reduces customer churn
- **Enable capacity for growth**: Increased uptime and throughput create headroom to take on additional orders without capital investment in new equipment
- **Improve competitive positioning**: Reliable delivery commitments can differentiate us in the market and support premium pricing or market share gains

This revenue impact is real but secondary to the direct operational efficiency gains, as it depends on converting improved reliability into customer acquisition and retention outcomes.
