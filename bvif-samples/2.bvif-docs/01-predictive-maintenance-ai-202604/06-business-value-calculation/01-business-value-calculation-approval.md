- Financial impact: 162 hours × $9,971 lost contribution margin per hour = $1,615,422 per quarter
- **Annualized: $6,461,688**
- **Data Sources**: Baseline downtime (324 hours Q4 2025) from Scenario document "Historical Performance: Unplanned Downtime Rate" section. Lost contribution margin per hour ($9,971) from "Historical Performance: Production Output Loss Rate During Downtime" section.

**2. Maintenance Cost Reduction ($1.73M annually)**
- Current: $12.00 per unit maintenance cost
- Target: $9.60 per unit maintenance cost
- Improvement: $2.40 savings per unit
- Financial impact: $2.40 × 720,000 units per year = $1,728,000
- **Annualized: $1,728,000**
- **Data Sources**: Baseline maintenance cost ($12.00/unit, $8,640,000 total for 720,000 units in 2025) from Scenario document "Historical Performance: Maintenance Cost per Production Unit" section. Cost breakdown showing $2,880,000 emergency reactive repair costs from same section.

**3. Delivery Penalty Avoidance ($1.17M annually)**
- Current: 90.0% OTD rate (234 late orders per quarter)
- Target: 95.0% OTD rate (117 late orders per quarter)
- Improvement: 117 fewer late orders per quarter
- Financial impact: 117 orders × $2,500 penalty per order = $292,500 per quarter
- **Annualized: $1,170,000**
- **Data Sources**: Baseline OTD rate (90.0%, 2,106 on-time out of 2,340 total Q4 2025) from Scenario document "Historical Performance: On-Time Delivery (OTD) rate" section. Contractual penalty ($2,500 per late order, $400K-$600K annually) from same section.
