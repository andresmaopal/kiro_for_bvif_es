# Initiative Definition — Predictive Maintenance using AI

## Who We Are
We are Precision Furniture Inc., a furniture manufacturer serving corporate clients with a strong presence across the United States. We operate three manufacturing sites with mostly automated production processes, enabling us to deliver high-quality furniture at scale.

## What We Propose
We propose implementing a predictive maintenance system powered by machine learning that analyzes equipment sensor data from our automated manufacturing lines. This AI-driven solution will predict equipment failures before they occur and optimize maintenance scheduling across our three facilities.

## Current State
Today, our maintenance approach is reactive or follows fixed schedules. When equipment fails unexpectedly, our production lines stop, creating unplanned downtime that disrupts our manufacturing capacity. This unpredictability makes it challenging to meet delivery commitments to our customers, impacting their experience and our reputation for reliability.

## Future State
With AI-powered predictive maintenance, we will shift from reactive to proactive equipment management. The system will continuously monitor sensor data, identify patterns indicating potential failures, and recommend optimal maintenance windows before breakdowns occur. This will minimize unplanned downtime, keep our production lines running smoothly, and enable us to fulfill delivery commitments consistently.

## Business Impact Areas
1. **Operational Efficiency**: Reduced unplanned equipment downtime and optimized maintenance schedules will increase our manufacturing capacity and throughput
2. **Customer Experience**: Improved delivery reliability and on-time fulfillment will strengthen customer satisfaction and trust
3. **Cost Management**: Preventing catastrophic equipment failures and optimizing maintenance activities will reduce emergency repair costs and extend equipment lifespan

## Budget Ownership
The budget for this initiative is owned by the **Chief Operating Officer (COO)**, as this initiative directly impacts operational efficiency, production capacity, and service delivery — all core responsibilities within the operations mandate. The predictable uptime and improved delivery performance align with operational excellence goals.
