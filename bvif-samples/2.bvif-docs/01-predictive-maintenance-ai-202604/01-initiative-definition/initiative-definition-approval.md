# Stage 1 — Initiative Definition Approval

## Initiative Definition Summary

**Who We Are**: Precision Furniture Inc., a furniture manufacturer serving corporate clients with a strong US presence, operating three manufacturing sites with mostly automated production processes.

**What We Propose**: Implementing a predictive maintenance system powered by machine learning that analyzes equipment sensor data to predict equipment failures and optimize maintenance scheduling.

**Current State**: Reactive or fixed-schedule maintenance approach leading to unexpected equipment failures, unplanned downtime, and challenges meeting delivery commitments.

**Future State**: Proactive equipment management with AI continuously monitoring sensor data, identifying potential failures, and recommending optimal maintenance windows to minimize downtime and ensure consistent delivery fulfillment.

**Business Impact Areas**:
1. Operational Efficiency — Reduced unplanned downtime and optimized maintenance schedules
2. Customer Experience — Improved delivery reliability and on-time fulfillment
3. Cost Management — Prevention of catastrophic failures and optimized maintenance activities

**Budget Ownership**: Chief Operating Officer (COO) or VP of Operations/Manufacturing
